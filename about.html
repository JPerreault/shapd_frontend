   What We're Up To

Our premise is simple: We're making it very, very easy for an average person to design, customize, and create jewelry. We happen to be using a technology called 3D printing to do that, which you might have heard about it in the news. However we take care of all the "production" - the actual making of the items - which means that you get to just focus on the fun stuff: designing or browsing beautiful jewelry. We're creating software to allow you to create anything you can imagine in an extremely easy way, and we're excited to get your input.
